Manual

Click on home to go to the generate receipts page

Click on settings button to go to the settings page

Click on history button see the history of the receipts generated

Follow the below steps to generate a receipt

Step 1: Fill in the values for `name` , `count`, `price` and `select Item type` in the `Receipt Generator`.

Step 2: Click `Add Item`

Step 3: Add all the items that you want to add to the basket

Step 4: Click on delete button in the `Shopping Basket` row, to delete an item from the shopping basket

Step 5: Once the items are added to the basket, you can preview your invoice by scrolling down the page or by clicking on the button `Show Invoice Preview`. To go back to the home page, click on the home icon on the top of the page.

Step 6: Click `Clear Basket` to clear all the items in the basket

Step 7: Click `save & print` to save the invoice and preview the print, click on print again in the preview page, to print the invoice. Once the `save & print` button is clicked, the invoice is saved in the db and the `shopping basket` is cleared as well as the invoice preview.

you will get an empty invoice generator after the invoice is printed.
